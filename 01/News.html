Obtaining an Afghanistan Passport

Coming soon...

Requirements for Passport Applicants:

1. Original Electronic Afghan National ID (Tazkira) or Paper Afghan National ID (Tazkira), certified by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Kabul. Please bring copy of it

2. Four Passport-Sized Photos: 4x5 cm in size.

3. Passport Fee:

- €120 for a five-year passport.
- €220 for a ten-year passport.

4. Personal Appearance: Applicants must appear in person at the Consular Section of the Embassy in Prague for the passport application process.

5. Accurate Completion of the Application Form: All forms must be filled out correctly. Please download the passport application form from here.

6. Biometric Process: Applicants must be present for the biometric process and electronic signature.

7. Proof of Residency: Applicants must provide valid immigration and residency documents from the host country.

8. For Minors Under the Age of 10: Applications must be submitted by their parents, accompanied by the child’s Tazkira or official birth certificate.

Payment and Submission:

If you meet all the above requirements, please transfer the passport fee to the Embassy of Afghanistan in Prague's bank account. After the payment is confirmed,
schedule an appointment to submit your documents and complete the application process here.

For further information, please contact the Embassy.

Visa

The Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan in Prague, Czech Republic issues visas to individuals residing in the Czech Republic and Slovakia.

Visa Application Process:

Visa applications can be submitted either in person at the embassy or by post.
Applications are processed in the order they are received by the Consular Section.
Incomplete applications or those missing necessary documentation will not be processed.
Make sure to prepare all required documents before submitting the visa application.

Visa Type:

Tourist Visa: Due to regulatory constraints, the Embassy can only issue single-entry tourist visas for three months, valid for a one-month stay.
Business Visa: If you are traveling for business or employment purposes, you must provide documentation of business, verifying the purpose and duration of your visit.
Entry Visa: For an entry visa, the relevant organization must obtain a Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MoFA) reference number from the Consular Department before applying at the Embassy.
Political and Services Visa: As per request 

Required Documents:

A fully completed and signed visa application form (download from here).
A passport with at least one blank page for the visa and a minimum of six months validity.
Two standard passport-sized photos with a white background.
Documents verifying your current address in the Czech Republic, Slovakia, or your home country.
A police clearance certificate (from the local authority) 

Visa Fees:

Tourist/Visit Visa - Single entry: 80 EUR + 1 EUR bank charge.
Entry visa - Three-month Duration, Single entry: 100 EUR + 1 EUR bank charge.
Business Visa - Single Entry: 3 months, 40 EUR, 6 Months 80 EUR.
Urgent tourist visa: 130 EUR + 1 EUR bank charge.
If you wish to apply for a visa, please transfer the applicable visa fee to the Embassy's account, plus an additional €1 for the bank charge, according to the type of visa you are applying here.

Visa Processing:

Processing Time: Once the Embassy receives the completed application with all necessary documents and payments, the visa will be issued within five to ten working days.
Urgent visa: An urgent visa will be processed within two working days.
Document Return: The applicant is responsible for collecting their passport and any original documents submitted.
Consular Section Receiving application working hours:
Application Submission: In-person applications can be submitted during the Consular Section's official opening hours, Monday to Friday, from 9:30 am to 12:30 pm.
