Visa

The Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan in Prague, Czech Republic issues visas to individuals residing in the Czech Republic and Slovakia.
Visa Application Process:
Visa applications can be submitted either in person at the embassy or by post.
Applications are processed in the order they are received by the Consular Section.
Incomplete applications or those missing necessary documentation will not be processed.
Make sure to prepare all required documents before submitting the visa application.

NOTE: Appointments are required only for getting a new passport. No appointment is required for all other services, included Visas.

Power of attorney

Power of Attorney for Marriage Certificate
Requirements:

One spouse residing abroad: request letter, Tazkira/passport copy, and two photos (4×4.5 cm).
Two adult witnesses: passport/Tazkira/driver’s license copies, and one photo (4×4.5 cm).
Legal Representative (Wakeel) in Afghanistan: Tazkira/passport copy, and one photo.
Other Power of Attorney Types
Requirements:
Clients: Tazkira/passport/driver’s license copy, and one photo (4×4.5 cm).
Two witnesses: passport/Tazkira/driver’s license copies, and one photo (4×4.5 cm).
Legal Representative: Tazkira/passport copy, and two photos.
For property cases: Certificate of Inheritance and property ownership details.

Marriage certificate

Marriage Certificate Issuance

The Embassy of Afghanistan in Prague issues marriage certificates (زوجیت خط) for already married couples. Both husband and wife must be present at the embassy along with five adult witnesses.

Requirements for Appointment:
- Both husband and wife must attend in person.
- Five adult witnesses (Iqrarkunendagan and Shahedaan).
- A request letter and required documents.

Please prepare the Marriage Certificate Form (فورم زوجیت خط) and all necessary documents prior to your appointment.
