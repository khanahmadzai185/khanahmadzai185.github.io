About the Embassy

Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan in Prague, Czech Republic

The Embassy of the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan in Prague serves as Afghanistan’s primary diplomatic representation in the Czech Republic. It manages bilateral relations and promotes Afghanistan's interests in the region, supporting cooperation across various fields, including trade, culture, and development.

Historical Relations with Czechoslovakia
Longstanding Cooperation since 1928

Afghanistan and the former Czechoslovakia share a long history of cooperation, dating back to 1928. A key milestone was the signing of a trade agreement on October 21, 1937, in Berlin, which greatly influenced Afghanistan’s infrastructure development. This partnership facilitated projects such as Kabul’s public transportation system and the establishment of industries like the Jabal al-Seraj and Herat cement factories.

Diplomatic and Educational Engagement

In 1949, Czechoslovakia opened its diplomatic mission in Afghanistan, which became an embassy on July 24, 1960. Despite the conflicts in Afghanistan, Czechoslovakia continued its support through educational programs for Afghan students and military training, especially for the Afghan Air Force.

Continued Relations with the Czech Republic and Slovakia
Post-1993 Relations

Following the dissolution of Czechoslovakia in 1993, Afghanistan continued strong diplomatic relations with both the Czech Republic and Slovakia. The Afghan Embassy in Prague remains instrumental in nurturing these ties, focusing on strengthening cooperation in areas such as culture, trade, and development.

Embassy’s Role and Services
Political, Economic, and Cultural Representation

Situated in the heart of Prague, the Afghan Embassy actively represents Afghanistan’s political, economic, and cultural interests in both the Czech Republic and Slovakia. The embassy plays a key role in maintaining and enhancing diplomatic relations between Afghanistan and these countries.

Consular Services

The embassy provides essential consular services, including:

Visa issuance for travelers to Afghanistan.
Passport renewals and extensions for Afghan citizens.
Assistance with legal matters such as birth certificates, police clearance, and other official documents.
Diplomatic Engagement and International Cooperation

The Afghan Embassy in Prague is engaged in diplomatic meetings, official functions, and collaborations with international organizations. Through these efforts, it promotes Afghanistan’s humanitarian, development, and economic goals, ensuring continued cooperation and communication between Afghanistan, the Czech Republic, and Slovakia.

Commitment to Afghan Nationals and Bilateral Relations
The Embassy of Afghanistan in Prague remains committed to supporting Afghan nationals abroad and fostering Afghanistan’s development. By promoting diplomatic ties and contributing to regional growth, the embassy strengthens the longstanding relationship between Afghanistan, the Czech Republic, and Slovakia.
